About Us

We travel around the world for share the emotions and discover our passion of the circus for lot of generations.

This is our story:

In 1975, Campbell brothers, street artists and acrobats from New York wish show a entertainment to lot of people.

They create the Campbell Circus. Their choices to present a show with originality and street of arts, musicians and animals, these eclectics choices will make their success.

After some years to travel, the circus is flourishing between sounds, acrobatics and animals training. The growing success, a new circus tent is acquired. The Campbell brothers and their company change the name to express their gratitude, the Wild Circus is born.

In 1979, The circus cooperates with the new internationals artists and integrates a great novelty:

The Light Show !

For theses years the Wild Circus has been fidelity to their identity and we continue to make dream through each show with our art.
